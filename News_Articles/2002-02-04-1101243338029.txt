


Martinez re-appointed to planning group





Date Published to Web:2002-02-04


The Alderwood City Council approved the request of Mayor Rex Luthor this past Monday night to re-appoint Raul Martinez to the city planning commission.
Martinez has served on the Alderwood Planning Commission since Jan. 31, 2000. His new term will expire Jan. 15, 2008.
"This appointment is forwarded to you (Council) with my full confidence in the appointee's abilities to fulfill the duties of a planning commissioner," wrote Luthor in his appointment letter to Council.
